01

Intelligent SMS Deal Detection

PromoBuddy uses Android's Notification Listener to automatically intercept and parse promotional SMS messages. It extracts the merchant name, discount amount, validity period, and category — then presents it as a clean deal card. OTP messages, bank transactions, and non-promotional texts are intelligently filtered out.

For Merchants

The Merchant Portal —
Your promotion command center.

A powerful web-based dashboard where merchants create, manage, and track promotions that reach thousands of PromoBuddy users instantly.

Create & Manage Campaigns

The Merchant Portal gives businesses complete control over their promotions. Create rich campaigns with AI-generated banner images, set validity periods, target specific categories, and track engagement — all from a clean, modern dashboard.

  • 🤖
    AI-Generated Banner Images — Describe your promotion and our LLM creates stunning, professional banner artwork instantly. No designer needed.
  • ⚡
    Quick Promotions — Create and publish flash deals in seconds. Set duration, discount, and push it live to the PromoBuddy app immediately.
  • 📊
    Campaign Analytics — Track views, engagement, and redemptions for every promotion. See which deals perform best.
  • 📱
    SMS Campaigns — Reach customers directly via SMS with promotional messages. Manage campaign templates and customer groups.
🎨

AI Image Generation

Type a description of your promotion and watch AI create eye-catching banner images in seconds. Choose from multiple generated options, or upload your own. Every promotion gets a professional look without the cost of a design team.

⚡

Quick Deal Creator

Need to push a flash sale? The Quick Deal creator lets you set up a time-limited promotion with just a title, discount, and duration. It goes live instantly in the app's flash carousel and triggers push notifications to nearby users.

👥

Customer Group Management

Organize your customer base into targeted groups. Send specific promotions to loyal customers, first-time visitors, or high-spenders. Define groups based on purchase history and engagement patterns.

✅

Auto-Approval & Moderation

Set up auto-approval rules for promotions that meet your criteria. Admins can review, approve, or reject submissions. Keep your platform quality high with built-in moderation workflows.

PromoBuddy is a complete deal discovery platform — a mobile app for consumers that auto-detects promotions from SMS messages, shows nearby deals on a map, and curates a personalized deal feed. It also includes a Merchant Portal where businesses create and manage AI-powered promotional campaigns.

PromoBuddy uses Android's Notification Listener Service to monitor incoming SMS notifications.
